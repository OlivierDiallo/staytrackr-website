Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒
Your data stays on your device. StayTrackr is built privacy-first. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Overview

StayTrackr ("the App") is built with privacy as a priority. Your data stays on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

2. Data We Collect

Data you enter (stored locally on your device only)

  • Property details (name, address, photos)
  • Booking information (dates, rates, notes)
  • Guest information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial data (income, expenses, rates)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it via the CSV export feature.

Anonymous analytics (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to understand how features are used. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal information, does not use cookies, and does not track you across apps or websites. Data is anonymised and aggregated. See telemetrydeck.com/privacy for details.

In-app purchases

Subscription and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

3. Data Storage

All your property, booking, guest and financial data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, StayTrackr schedules local notifications on your device for booking arrivals and departures. These are processed entirely on-device and never sent to external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to any third party. The only data that leaves your device is the anonymous, aggregated analytics described in section 2.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

StayTrackr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: your@email.com
