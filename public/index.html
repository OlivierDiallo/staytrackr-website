Everything you need

Built for serious
property owners

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. All your rental data in one beautiful, private app.

📅

Visual Calendar

See every booking at a glance on a color-coded month grid. Tap any day to filter, or tap a date to pre-fill a new booking instantly.

💰

Income & Expense Tracking

Log nightly rates, platform fees, cleaning costs, repairs, utilities, and more. Per-property breakdowns keep your numbers clean.

📊

Totals & Analytics

Monthly, yearly, or all-time summaries. Occupancy %, average nightly rate, net profit — all calculated automatically.

🏘

Multi-Property

Manage unlimited properties each with their own currency, color, emoji, mortgage tracking, and commission %. Fully isolated data.

👥

Guest CRM

A full guest database with contact info, stay history, and smart sorting by name, most stays, or property. Add guests inline while booking.

🔁

Recurring Bills

Set up monthly recurring expenses like insurance, HOA, or management fees. Convert them to actual expense entries with a single swipe.

🏦

Mortgage & ROI

Enter your mortgage details and see a live ROI panel — tracking how much you've earned vs. paid, and your equity progress over time.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get reminded before guest arrivals and departures. Configurable advance notice so you're always prepared for check-in day.

📤

CSV Export

Export bookings, expenses, or a combined report as CSV. Tax season sorted in seconds. Share via any app directly from Settings.
